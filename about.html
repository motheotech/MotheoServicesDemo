About Motheo Services

Rooted in Orange Farm, growing with the community through technology

Our Story

Motheo Services was founded in Drieziek, Orange Farm by Naledi Motheo with a vision to bring quality tech services to our community. What started as a small IT support service has grown into a comprehensive tech solutions provider.

We understand the unique challenges of township life and are committed to providing affordable, reliable tech services while creating opportunities for local youth through skills development.
